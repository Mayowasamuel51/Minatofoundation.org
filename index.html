Welcome to

Minato Foundation

We are dedicated to improving academic, career, and professional opportunities for individuals from underserved backgrounds. At Minato Foundation, we believe in empowering communities through education and career development. Join us as we work together to create a brighter future.

Our Programs

Scholarship Program

Our scholarship program provides financial aid to students from underserved backgrounds, enabling them to pursue higher education and achieve their dreams.

Mentorship Program

We connect individuals with experienced professionals in their fields of interest, fostering guidance, networking, and growth.

Workshops and Training

We offer workshops on topics such as resume building, interview preparation, and skill development to prepare individuals for the workforce.
